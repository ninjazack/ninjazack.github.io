Welcome

I am Zeqian Yu, also known as Zeq, a master's student in the Master in Economics: Empirical Application and Policies at the University of the Basque Country (UPV/EHU). From the 2026-2027 academic year, I will join the MRes in Economic Analysis (path to PhD) at Universidad Carlos III de Madrid.

My research interests are in macroeconomics and international finance, especially policy-relevant questions related to monetary policy, financial markets, and international economic linkages. I am also interested in empirical research designs and modern quantitative tools, including causal inference, artificial intelligence, and machine learning, for studying economic and financial questions.
